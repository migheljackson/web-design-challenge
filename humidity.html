Latitude and Humidity  

This visualization is not as clear cut as max temperature.  These humdiity observations taken in January show that there appear to be more cities with high humditiy in the Northern latitudes.          

Perhaps this a result of the Northern latitudes being slightly more rainy in the winter months.  Ineterstingly, there are also several Northern cities that appear to have 0% humidity.  It is difficult to tell if that is an accurate measurement, or if there was no data collected there.
