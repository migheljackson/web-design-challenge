Latitude and Wind Speed  

This visualization shows wind speed observations as they relate to distance from the equator.  Surprisingly, there is not much difference in wind speed regardless of distance from the equator.  Most cities appear to fall within a range of 0-10 mph.                  

It is notable that looking at the outliers yields some differences.  In the northernmost latitudes, there are cities that approach over 35 mph winds.  Ostensibly those are cities that are near the North Pole where conditions can be fairly unforgiving.
