Latitude and Max Temperature  

In this visualization, we can see that max temperature in cities goes up as we move towards the equator and eventually plateaus around 90 degrees, with a few outliers.        

These observations were made in January, so as we can see the curve is not a perfect rainbow from one end of the latitude scale to the other.  This is likely due to these observations being taken during what is typically summer in the Southern Hemisphere, so the high temperatures are higher than what we see in the Northern latitudes, which are in winter.
